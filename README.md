# Olist-E-Commerce-Sales-Customer-Analytics-Dashboard
End-to-end Power BI Business Intelligence project analyzing 103K+ e-commerce orders, 99K+ customers, sales performance, customer retention, payment behavior, and delivery logistics across Brazil. Built using Power BI, DAX, Power Query, and SQL to generate actionable business insights and strategic recommendations.

## Project Overview

The Olist E-Commerce Dashboard provides a comprehensive analysis of business operations by examining four critical areas:

📈 Business Performance
💰 Sales & Revenue Analysis
👥 Customer Behaviour Analysis
🚚 Delivery & Logistics Analysis

The objective is to identify growth opportunities, customer retention challenges, revenue drivers, and operational bottlenecks while delivering executive-level insights through interactive visualizations.

## 🎯 Business Objectives

This project was designed to answer the following business questions:

## Revenue & Growth
How has revenue evolved over time?
What is the Year-over-Year growth rate?
Which states contribute the most revenue?
## Product Performance
Which product categories generate the highest revenue?
Which categories drive business growth?
## Customer Analytics
What percentage of customers return for repeat purchases?
What payment methods are most preferred?
How satisfied are customers with their purchases?
## Logistics & Operations
How efficient is the delivery network?
What are the freight cost patterns?


## 💡 Strategic Recommendations
Customer Growth
Launch loyalty programs
Introduce personalized marketing
Improve post-purchase engagement
Sales Expansion
Expand into underperforming states
Promote high-margin categories
Increase cross-selling opportunities
Logistics Optimization
Reduce delivery lead times
Improve route planning
Establish regional fulfillment centers
Payment Strategy
Continue supporting installment payments
Introduce customer financing incentives
